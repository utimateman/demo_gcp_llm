Table Name: Patient Admission Records
Description: This table contains records of patient admissions to the hospital, including patient details, admission dates, medical conditions, assigned doctors, and wards.

Columns:
- Patient ID: Unique identifier for each patient. (Integer)
- Patient Name: Name of the patient admitted. (String)
- Admission Date: Date when the patient was admitted to the hospital. (Date)
- Medical Condition: Condition for which the patient was admitted. (String)
- Assigned Doctor: Doctor assigned to oversee the patient's care. (String)
- Ward: Hospital ward where the patient is admitted. (String)

File Format: CSV
Delimiter: Comma (,)
