Use-Case Name: Patient Admission Analysis
Objective: Analyze patient admission data to understand trends, optimize resource allocation, and improve patient care.
Business Context:
- The hospital aims to improve operational efficiency by analyzing admission patterns, such as peak admission times and common medical conditions.
- Insights from this analysis will help in better staffing decisions, ensuring adequate resources in high-demand wards.
- Understanding patient demographics and medical conditions will aid in personalized patient care and treatment planning.
- Regular analysis will monitor trends in readmission rates and patient outcomes, aiming for continuous improvement in healthcare services.
Data Source:
- Patient Admission Records: Contains detailed records of patient admissions, including patient demographics, medical conditions, and assigned doctors.
- Format: CSV
- Frequency: Updated daily
